Baked Onion

This often-overlooked recipe is a great addition to any savory meal. Try it alond with the peasant chicken recipe.

Easy to make and wrapped in simple notes of elegance, the baked onion will surprise you with its profile.

    Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter, plus extra for the pan
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

    Preparation Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Grease a square 8-inch Pyrex baking dish with butter.
  3. Peel the onion and slice it into Â¼-inch-thick slices, as shown in the video below. You will probably end up with five slices.
  4. Arrange the slices in a single layer in the prepared baking dish. Melt the butter and pour it all over the onions. Sprinkle with Kosher salt.
  5. Roast the onions until the bottoms are golden, about 20 minutes.
  6. Turn the onion slices to the other side and bake them until golden-brown, about 20 more minutes.
  7. Serve with your favorie savory dish!
